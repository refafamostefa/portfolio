Our Services

Services

Financial Audit & Judicial Expertise

We provide essential oversight through Statutory Audits and specialized contractual financial audits to verify the integrity and compliance of your annual accounts. As a Judicial Expert in Finance and Accounting, M. REFAFA also ensures the rigorous review and cleanup of accounts and oversees mandatory year-end inventory missions.

Accounting System Implementation & Bookkeeping

Our missions focus on establishing structured and compliant accounting systems for your entity, including the development of an accounting plan and the determination of appropriate procedures. We manage all aspects of ongoing bookkeeping, including the registration of daily operations, establishing capital balances, and maintaining all required legal registers.

Fiscal Strategy & Dispute Management

We ensure complete fiscal compliance by preparing and establishing all required tax declarations and annual salary reports. We are ready to provide expert assistance during official fiscal verification processes and handle the professional management of complex fiscal disputes and litigation.

Social Compliance & Payroll Management

Our social assistance services focus on accurate labor regulation compliance, specifically covering routine personnel management. This includes the monthly establishment of pay slips and the rigorous holding and maintenance of required payroll journals and individual employee accounts.

Annual Legal Secretariat

We provide crucial legal assistance to ensure your company meets all annual administrative and regulatory requirements. This involves preparing for Board Meetings and General Assemblies, drafting official activity reports and minutes (PVs), and depositing your annual accounts.

Management Consulting & Performance Control

We help enhance internal control by setting up specialized analytical accounting for accurate cost calculation and optimizing commercial management of sales and stock. This service utilizes preliminary studies and creates customized corporate dashboards to provide actionable management ratios and commentary for strategic planning.

Business Creation & Lifecycle Management

We provide expert support throughout your entire business journey, assisting as an accompagnateur des initiateurs de projet and managing the formal creation of new companies. When a business cycle concludes, we handle the cessation of activity, including the meticulous monitoring of liquidation processes. This ensures compliance and provides you with the required closing reports and final liquidation balance sheets.

Financial Dossier Elaboration & Project Support

We offer essential assistance in the economic and financial sphere, specializing in the professional preparation of complex documentation. This includes elaborating comprehensive technico-economic dossiers and preparing detailed applications for bank credit dossiers to secure financing.
